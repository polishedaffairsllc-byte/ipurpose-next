# Identity Lab (MVP)

## Purpose
Clarify identity structure through three maps:
- Self‑Perception
- Self‑Concept
- Self‑Narrative

## Self‑Perception Map
- How I describe myself when no one is listening
- How I feel in groups
- How I feel alone
- How I experience my presence
- How I experience my emotions
- How I experience my thoughts
- How I experience my body
- How I experience my voice

## Self‑Concept Map
- I believe I am someone who…
- I believe my role in the world is…
- I believe I am good at…
- I believe I am bad at…
- I believe I am meant for…
- I believe I am not meant for…
- I believe people see me as…
- I believe my value comes from…

## Self‑Narrative Map
- The story of my childhood is…
- The story of my education is…
- The story of my work is…
- The story of my relationships is…
- The story of my failures is…
- The story of my successes is…
- The story of my struggles is…
- The story of my strengths is…
