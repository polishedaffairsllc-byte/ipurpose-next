# Meaning Lab (MVP)

## Purpose
Build meaning coherence through three maps:
- Value Structure
- Coherence Structure
- Direction Structure

## Value Structure
- I care deeply about…
- I feel alive when…
- I feel empty when…
- I feel most myself when…
- I feel disconnected when…
- I protect…
- I fight for…
- I refuse to compromise on…

## Coherence Structure
- My life feels meaningful when…
- My life feels meaningless when…
- I feel grounded when…
- I feel lost when…
- Things make sense to me when…
- I feel confused when…
- I feel aligned when…
- I feel fragmented when…

## Direction Structure
- I feel drawn toward…
- I feel called to…
- I move away from…
- I resist…
- I feel pulled forward by…
- I feel held back by…
- I feel inspired by…
- I feel drained by…
