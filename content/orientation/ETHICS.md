# Ethics & Community Charter

## Ethical Foundation
- Dignity of every human
- Autonomy protection
- Non-exploitation
- Non-coercion
- Non-manipulation
- Non-dependency
- Informed consent
- Transparency
- Non-extraction
- Non-domination

## What This Space Is Not
- No guru narratives
- No savior dynamics
- No charisma hierarchy
- No pressure-based participation
- No emotional extraction

## Data Ethics
- Consent-first data use
- No surveillance
- No behavioral manipulation
- Privacy by default

## Community Conduct
- Speak from lived experience
- Protect confidentiality
- Listen to understand
- Choose curiosity over judgment
