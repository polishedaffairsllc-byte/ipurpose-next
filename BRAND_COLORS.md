# iPurpose Brand Colors — Canonical Reference

This is the authoritative color palette for iPurpose. All design and development should reference these values.

---

## **#4b4e6d – Deep Indigo Slate**
**Role:** Anchor  
**Meaning:** Wisdom, seriousness, structure  

**Use for:**
- Primary text
- Navigation
- Anything that needs authority or containment

**Voice:** "You're safe here. I'm not guessing."

---

## **#9c88ff – Soft Electric Lavender**
**Role:** Insight  
**Meaning:** Intuition, creativity, future-thinking  

**Use for:**
- Highlights
- Active states
- AI / Compass / insight moments

**Voice:** "There's something more here—pay attention."

---

## **#fcc4b7 – Warm Peach Blush**
**Role:** Heart  
**Meaning:** Compassion, welcome, softness  

**Use for:**
- Gentle CTAs
- Emotional reflection spaces
- Supportive UI moments

**Voice:** "You're allowed to be human here."

---

## **#88b04b – Grounded Green**
**Role:** Growth  
**Meaning:** Growth-with-roots, healthy green (not hustle green)  

**Use for:**
- Progress indicators
- Completion states
- "You're moving forward" signals

**Voice:** "This is sustainable. Keep going."

---

## **#fff3da – Cream Light**
**Role:** Breathing Room  
**Meaning:** Softness, space, calm  

**Use for:**
- Backgrounds
- Containers
- Reflection zones

**Voice:** "Slow down. There's space."

---

## **#d4af37 – Classic Gold**
**Role:** Value  
**Meaning:** Earned worth (not flash)  

**Use sparingly for:**
- Milestones
- Founder-level moments
- Sacred emphasis

**Voice:** "This matters."

---

## **#e6c87c – Soft Gold Sand**
**Role:** Bridge  
**Meaning:** Warmth meets prestige  

**Use for:**
- Secondary highlights
- Subtle dividers
- Elevated accents

**Voice:** "You've arrived, and you're still grounded."

---
